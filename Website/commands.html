Complete guide to all commands and features

Remyndrs understands natural language, so just text like you normally would. Here are all the ways you can use it:

‚è∞ Setting Reminders

Get reminded at exactly the right time

Specific Date & Time

"Remind me to call mom tomorrow at 2pm"
"Remind me about the dentist appointment on March 15 at 10am"

You can use dates, times, or both

Relative Time

"Remind me to check the oven in 30 minutes"

"Remind me every Monday to take out the trash"

SNOOZE 1h

Reply SNOOZE within 30 minutes of receiving a reminder to snooze it. Default is 15 minutes, or specify a time like "1h" or "30m"

üí° Pro Tips

Get the most out of Remyndrs

‚úÖ Natural Language Works

Don't worry about exact phrasing. "Remind me tomorrow" and "Set a reminder for tomorrow" both work!

‚úÖ Be Specific

The more details you give, the better. "Remind me about doctor" vs "Remind me about Dr. Smith appointment on Tuesday at 3pm"

‚úÖ One Message Per Action

For best results, send separate texts for different tasks. Instead of "Remind me about X and also add Y to my list", send two messages.
