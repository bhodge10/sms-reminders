Your Privacy Matters

What We Collect

We only collect what's necessary to provide the service:

  • Your phone number (to send/receive messages)
  • Your reminder text and scheduled times
  • Basic usage data to improve the service

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Send you the reminders you request
  • Improve our natural language processing
  • Provide customer support if needed

How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • No Selling: We never sell your data. Ever.
  • No Ads: We don't serve ads or share data with advertisers
  • Minimal Retention: Completed reminders are automatically deleted after 90 days
